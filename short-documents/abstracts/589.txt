    This thesis project, focusing on the arrangement, description and cataloguing of the Nancy Newhall negative collection at George Eastman House, was undertaken to provide accessibility to the collection for research purposes. The paper includes the following information: (1) A short biography of the life of Nancy Hewhall and a chronology of her life outlining major events and achievements; and (2) an outline and discussion of the arrangement, rehousing, digitization, and cataloguing of the collection.
